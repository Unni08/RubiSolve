First Layer

  • Solve any one of the sides(color). White is preferable. 
  • Keep the solved side upwards.
  • Now if you see any corners with same color together forming a line, keep the line to your left and follow this algorithm. 
    R U2 R' U' R U2 L' U R' U' L
  • If you don't see anything like that the follow this algorithm(No setup required)-
     F R U' R' U' R U R' F' R U R' U' R' F R F'
  • Do any 1 accordingly and keep white downwards.

    -Keeping the solved piece towards you and one top is facing you in your right= R U R' U  R U2 R'
    -Keeping the solved piece towards you and one top is facing you in your left= L' U' L U' L' U2 L

2 Piece

  • There are 3 situations
  •  2 unsolved pieces are together and facing same direction. Keep the top color towards you.-
    R2 D R' U2 R D' R' U2 R'
  • 2 unsolved pieces are together and facing opposite directions. Keep the solved part facing to your right and face to the bottom (white, if you solved it first)-
    L U R' U' L' U R U' and hold the cube the normal way
  • 2 unsolved pieces are separated, keep the in such a way that the top color faces you and its in your left and face to the bottom (white, if you solved it first)-
    R' U R D' R' U' R D
